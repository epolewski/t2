Grenade

A grenade is an explosive device typically used in military and tactical settings. In Terminator 2: Judgement Day, one such grenade, referred to as a "Blooper," plays a role in the climactic battle between the Terminators and the protagonists.

During the confrontation at Cyberdyne Systems Corporation's laboratory, the T-1000 (Terminator) manages to grab hold of a grenade—the so-called "Blooper"—which it uses against its adversaries. The last grenade is acquired by the T-1000 after impaling itself on a steel bar, ultimately leading to its own destruction.
