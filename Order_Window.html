Order Window

Description

An order window is a point of interaction between customers and the establishment where they can place their food orders. Typically, an order window features a menu listing various dishes or meals available at the establishment.

Staff

At an order window, a staff member known as the Window Woman works to assist customers in placing their orders. She typically engages with customers, providing information about the offered food options and taking their choices for preparation.

Function

An order window plays a crucial role in streamlining the process of ordering food and ensuring smooth customer service within establishments such as restaurants, diners, or fast-food joints.
