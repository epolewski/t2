T-1000/Guard

The T-1000/Guard is a character portrayed as an android disguised as a Guard in the film Terminator 2: Judgement Day. This entity possesses exceptional abilities of shape-shifting and deception, making it ideal for tasks that require stealth and manipulation.

Characteristics

In one scene, the T-1000/Guard deceives a Nurse by pretending to place its weapon in a locker while secretly hiding it within its chest. It subsequently navigates through the Isolation Security Checkpoint, followed by another instance where it manages to pass by an Attendant unnoticed.
