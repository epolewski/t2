Plasma Screen

Overview

A plasma screen is a type of display technology that uses gas-filled cells filled with neon and xenon gases. These cells are sandwiched between two sheets of Glass, creating an electrically charged environment in which the gas can be manipulated to create images.

Use in Terminator 2: Judgement Day

In the movie Terminator 2: Judgement Day, John Connor utilizes a plasma screen to view account details related to Skynet's weapons and Cyberdyne Systems.

Security Concerns

The film highlights the potential security risks associated with plasma screens, as they display sensitive information like PIN numbers. The use of these screens in high-security environments raises concerns about data leaks and vulnerabilities.
