Charon Mesa

Location

Charon Mesa is situated approximately 2 miles from Charon Mesa and about 15 miles away from Calexico. It features a desert-style landscape with an assortment of trash, abandoned vehicles, and other debris scattered around the area.

Features

The site includes a dirt airstrip behind the trailers that surround the oasis, as well as a stripped Huey Helicopter sitting on blocks nearby. The environment appears to be largely dusty and desolate.
