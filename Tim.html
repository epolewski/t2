Tim

Tim is a thirteen-year-old Hispanic boy and a close friend of John Connor. He is seen as supportive towards John in his disagreement with Janella Voight, often offering comfort and camaraderie during their shared experiences.

Characteristics

As an ally to John, Tim accompanies him on various adventures and witnesses some thrilling moments. He questions John about his real mother but receives no clear answer from the protagonist.

Pivotal Moments

Tim plays an essential role when he keeps a lookout for John while he carries out suspicious activities at the ready-teller Machine. This highlights his loyalty and trust in John's actions.
