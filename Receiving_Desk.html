Receiving Desk

Functionality

A receiving desk is a crucial component of any Hospital, designed to provide a secure entry point for both staff and patients. It typically features a Glass window that separates the reception area from the main Hospital Corridor.

In Fiction

In the popular science fiction film series Terminator, the receiving desk serves as an essential location where various characters interact with Hospital staff members. In particular, the T-1000 (an advanced android assassin) uses this location while searching for SARAH.html">Sarah Connor.
