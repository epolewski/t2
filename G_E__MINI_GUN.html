G.E. MINI-GUN

Description

The G.E. MINI-GUN is a powerful anti-personnel weapon that was widely used during the Vietnam War era. In the movie Terminator 2, this weapon is prominently featured when the character Terminator (T-800) picks it up and asks if he can use it.

Operation

The G.E. Mini-gun feeds rounds of ammunition from a canister located next to the gun's barrel. This allows for continuous firing without the need for manual reloading.
