Los Angeles

Introduction

Los Angeles is a major City in California, known for its sprawling size and diverse culture. It's also the setting of many popular films and television series, including the Terminator franchise.

Dissolve

In the Terminator universe, Los Angeles becomes the site of severe destruction due to the nuclear Explosion initiated by Skynet's weapons.
