Night Nurse

The Night Nurse is a character featured in the film Terminator 2: Judgement Day. She works during night shifts at Pescadero State Hospital for the Criminally Insane, where SARAH.html">Sarah Connor and other patients are held.

Role

As a night Nurse, she ensures the well-being of patients throughout her shift. During one particular instance, she encounters the T-1000/Guard while it walks past her without noticing her presence. She later checks his gun, which he takes into a secured area.
