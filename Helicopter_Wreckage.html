Helicopter Wreckage

In Terminator 2: Judgment Day, after the Helicopter crashes into the Tanker truck, it becomes a crumpled mess of twisted metal.

The wreckage is situated behind the Tanker truck, barely visible from the main road. During this scene, T-1000 drives a blade through a man's abdomen while walking past the destroyed Helicopter without slowing down or showing any interest in the ruined vehicle.
