Neural-net Processor

Definition

A neural-net processor is a computer system designed to mimic human thought processes and learning capabilities. It operates at room temperature, making it distinct from other computers that require superconductivity for optimal functioning.

Impact on Technology

The neural-net processor has the potential to transform various aspects of daily life due to its ability to think and learn like humans without getting tired or making mistakes. Miles Dyson, the narrator's husband, believes this innovation will revolutionize technology.
