The Real-Time Image

In Terminator 2: Judgment Day, "The Real-Time Image" is an effect that freezes and replays a moment, showcasing the facial expression of a teenage guy grinning in slow motion. This sequence focuses on the guy's lips, visually displaying the shape and movement of them as they form a smile.

This unique visual representation serves to offer symbolic data on human emotions such as smiling, while also adding a surreal aspect to the film's overall aesthetic.
