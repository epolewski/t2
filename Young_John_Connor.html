Young John Connor

Characteristics

The ten-year-old Young John Connor is a character in the film Terminator 2: Judgement Day. He has an interest in working on his Honda 125 dirtbike carburetor. Presented with ripped Levi's and long stringy hair, he possesses notable intelligence and sharp-eyed observations.

Personality Traits

Aside from his technical inclination, Young John Connor displays a rebellious nature, often making it difficult for Janella Voight to call him by her choice of name.
