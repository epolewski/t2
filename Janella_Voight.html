Janella Voight

Janella Voight is the foster mother of John Connor, portrayed by actress Bess Motta. In the Terminator franchise, Janella takes on the role of a strict and demanding caretaker who expects her foster son, John, to maintain a tidy living space.

Characterization

As a foster parent, Janella can be seen as an authoritative figure in John's life. Her primary concern revolves around his living conditions and personal hygiene, which she demonstrates through her frequent insistence that he clean up his room.
