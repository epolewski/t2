Main Gallery

In the Terminator series, the Main Gallery is a significant location where events unfold. It serves as the backdrop for various scenes and encounters in the movies.

Terminator 2: Judgement Day

The Main Gallery is featured in Terminator 2: Judgement Day. The scene involves a battle between the T-1000 and SWAT Team members using Machine guns, tear gas launchers, and Kevlar Vests.
