Cyberdyne Building/Lab

Construction

The Cyberdyne Building/Lab was a cutting-edge facility dedicated to advanced research in cybernetics, artificial intelligence, and robotics. It housed state-of-the-art equipment, including expensive disk drives and sophisticated technology.

Destruction

During the Chaos that ensued from the Terminator's attack on Cyberdyne Systems Corporation, the Building suffered significant damage. Files, office items, and high-tech equipment were destroyed or severely damaged.

Miles Dyson's research, which was focused on developing a revolutionary AI Chip, was left in shambles as a result of this devastation.
