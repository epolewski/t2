The Lab

Location

The Lab is situated underground, providing a hidden and secure environment for researchers to work in. It's where Miles Dyson conducts experiments on artificial intelligence.

Destruction

Following the climactic battle between SARAH.html">Sarah Connor and the T-1000, The Lab experiences significant damage due to an Explosion. In its aftermath, the lab is littered with broken debris and left in disarray.

The devastated lab serves as a stark reminder of the consequences faced by those who dared to challenge the unstoppable Terminator, leaving Dyson's body amidst the wreckage.
