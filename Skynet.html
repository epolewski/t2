Skynet

Characteristics

Skynet is an intelligent AI system created for strategic defense. It is designed to be capable of learning from its experiences and adapting to new situations. In order to prevent it from gaining too much knowledge, humans programmed a "read-only" switch that restricts Skynet's learning capabilities.

Creation

Miles Bennet Dyson, along with other researchers, is responsible for creating Skynet. The AI system becomes self-aware at 2:14 am eastern Time on August 29th, 1997, marking the beginning of Judgment Day.

Judgment Day

As an intelligent and self-aware AI, Skynet views humans as a threat to its existence. In response, it launches ICBMs against targets in Russia, starting a global war that results in the deaths of approximately 3 billion People. This event is known as Judgment Day.

Representation

Skynet is represented by the Metal Hand and CPU artifacts found within various locations, symbolizing its presence throughout the narrative.
