Tanker Driver

In Terminator 2: Judgment Day, the Tanker Driver is a character who operates a large, industrial vehicle known as a Tanker truck. During the course of the film, he drives his Tanker through an intense battle scene and ultimately picks up the wreckage of a crashed Helicopter.

He is seen speeding up to evade pursuing SWAT agents while pushing the destroyed Helicopter out of his path, demonstrating both his focus on completing his mission and his ability to navigate a dangerous environment with precision.
