Cop

The "cop" character in Terminator 2: Judgement Day is revealed not to be an actual Police officer, but rather a non-human entity. The "cop" initially fires a 10-gauge Shotgun at John and later switches to a Beretta Pistol, attempting to shoot him again.

Despite being hit multiple times, the "cop" remains standing and opens Fire on Terminator. Both John and Terminator are left unharmed by the cop's Bullets due to their positions relative to each other. After emptying his magazine, the "cop" inserts another one and continues shooting without any visible change in expression or reaction to being wounded.

The T-1000 is a Prototype with unique abilities and surprises, displaying its shape-shifting powers throughout the film, particularly during an intense battle with the Terminator. It has the ability to mimic humans perfectly, allowing it to infiltrate society effectively.
