Artifact (Ceramic Rectangle)

Description

The Artifact (Ceramic Rectangle) is a small, liver-colored ceramic rectangle that has been shattered and later reconstructed. It was mounted on a metal frame for display purposes. The artifact was stored inside a sealed container filled with inert gas to preserve its integrity.

Handling Protocols

To maintain the pristine condition of the Ceramic Rectangle, it was handled with great care by Miles Dyson, who treated it similarly to how the Turin Shroud is handled - with utmost respect and caution.

Storage Conditions

The Artifact (Ceramic Rectangle) was stored in a stainless steel cabinet, which further protected it from external damage or contaminants. This exemplifies the importance Cyberdyne Systems placed on preserving this artifact.
