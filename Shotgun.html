Shotgun

A sawn-off 10-GAUGE WINCHESTER LEVER-ACTION SHOTGUN owned by Lloyd. It is used as a weapon by both the "Cop" (played by Michael Biehn) and the Terminator throughout the course of the film.

  • The shotgun is fired multiple times before it becomes empty.
  • It is used to attack John Connor, portrayed by Edward Furlong, who is threatened with the shotgun during his encounter with the "Cop."
  • The shotgun also plays a crucial role in the fight between the Terminator and the T-1000, as it is used to fend off the advancing T-1000 and Orderlies.
  • Later on, the Terminator uses this weapon to jam the shotgun through the gap in closing Doors to prevent entry into the room where John Connor is held captive.
  • During the climactic battle, the Terminator fires slugs and buckshots at the T-1000, eventually severing its hook-hand.
