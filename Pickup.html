Pickup

In Terminator 2: Judgement Day, a Pickup Truck plays a significant role in the storyline as the primary mode of transportation for protagonists John Connor (Edward Furlong) and Terminator (Robert Patrick).

After their Van crashes, the two find themselves using a pickup to continue their journey. The vehicle is driven by John, with Terminator sliding SARAH.html">Sarah into the front seat and climbing into the back. As they drive away from danger, the pickup faces several obstacles, including being hit multiple times by an oncoming Tanker truck and engaging in various action-packed scenes involving characters leaping onto it.

The pickup's driver is described as an elderly Hispanic man in his 50s, who eventually allows himself to be pushed by the T-1000 (T-10000) while John remains in control of the vehicle without dictating its direction.
