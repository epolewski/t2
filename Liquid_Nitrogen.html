Liquid Nitrogen

Liquid Nitrogen is a highly volatile substance that plays a significant role in Terminator 2: Judgement Day. It is transported in a Tanker, with the potential to cause damage when released.

  • The liquid nitrogen is stored within a Tanker truck, which has a windshield shattered by Gunfire.
  • When the windshield is shattered, the liquid nitrogen explodes against the front bulkhead of the Tanker truck, escaping and creating a comet-tail of nitrogen vapor.
  • Following this Explosion, the Tanker spills its contents, causing significant danger for the characters in the movie's plot.

Effect on Plot

The presence of liquid nitrogen significantly impacts the events that transpire within Terminator 2: Judgement Day. It becomes crucial to the storyline when it is used as a weapon by John Connor against the T-1000 model Terminator. Furthermore, it contributes to the chaotic atmosphere and tension throughout the film, particularly during the climactic battle at the Steel Mill.
