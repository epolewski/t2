Jock-types

In Terminator 2: Judgment Day, two college-aged men, referred to as "jock-types," are seen across the street from where John Connor is being kidnapped. They attempt to assist him when they notice that a robot (the Terminator) is standing with one leg up.

When the jock-types approach John and inquire about his wellbeing, he quickly dismisses them, suspecting their intentions may not be genuine. This interaction highlights the tense atmosphere surrounding John's life during this Time.
