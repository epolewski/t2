LAPD Black-and-White

An LAPD Black-and-White is a Police vehicle commonly used by law enforcement agencies. In the storyline of Terminator 2: Judgment Day, it plays a significant role as a tool for the T-1000 (Terminator) to navigate through Los Angeles, California.

The LAPD Black-and-White is employed when the security Guard raises the barricade at the Great Military Leader's home. The Terminator (T-1000) subsequently drives this car throughout the City, using it as his primary means of transportation during his pursuit of John Connor and SARAH.html">Sarah Connor.
