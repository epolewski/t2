Time

In the realm of Los Angeles, time is characterized by a mixture of various events, occurrences, and experiences. It spans across diverse locations, such as playgrounds, arcade rooms, bank parking lots, Hospital corridors, and military installations.

Events

The passage of time in this world is filled with numerous events, including Police chases, scientific experiments, guerrilla warfare, life-threatening encounters, and unexpected friendships. Despite the challenges faced by its inhabitants, they persist through time to shape their environment and fate.

Legacy

Time's legacy within Los Angeles can be seen in its ever-evolving narrative, as it witnesses the growth and development of Humanity while battling an impending doom. Its characters are shaped by the decisions they make during this critical period, ultimately defining their destiny.
