Guerrilla General

Appearance

The Guerrilla General, often referred to as "CONNOR," sports a distinctive attire in the form of a military uniform and a black beret. He carries night-vision binoculars to observe the intense battles from a distance.

Role

The Guerrilla General is an observer who remains at the fringes of the Battlefield, watching the unfolding events through his night-vision equipment. His presence serves as a symbolic representation of John Connor's role in overseeing the war against Skynet's forces.
