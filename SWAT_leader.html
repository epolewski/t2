SWAT leader

The SWAT leader is a character from the movie Terminator 2: Judgment Day. He leads the SWAT Team during their assault on Cyberdyne Systems Corporation.

During the operation, he crouches in the fog while white-knuckling his Rifle. Despite being equipped with protective gear like a gas mask, he is eventually shot in the leg by the Terminator, forcing him to drop his weapon and gas mask.

He struggles to breathe, choking and gagging on the ground as the scene unfolds around him.
