Cops

In Terminator 2: Judgment Day, cops are depicted as well-trained and highly efficient law enforcement personnel. They arrive at Pescadero State Hospital for the Criminally Insane in response to an emergency.

Upon their arrival, they don't notice a Terminator walking among them, which highlights the effectiveness of its camouflage abilities. However, as soon as it exits through the window, they start shooting at it.

During the scene, the cops use various equipment such as body armor, gas masks, M-16s, tear-gas launchers, and ropes.
