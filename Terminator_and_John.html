Terminator and John

In Terminator 2: Judgment Day, Terminator (T-1000) accompanies John Connor on a mission to save his mother, SARAH.html">Sarah, who is incarcerated at Pescadero State Hospital for the Criminally Insane.

Upon arrival at Miles Dyson's House, they find themselves under attack by T-1000, which has infiltrated their intended target. As they sprint towards the House, gunshots are fired but remain muffled due to the distance and exterior environment.
