9mm Bullets

In the Terminator series, 9mm Bullets are used as a primary weapon by John Connor and other characters throughout the films. They cause bullet holes in the Terminator's leather Jacket and metal endoskeleton.

When fired at the Terminator, these Bullets flatten against its armored endoskeleton before being absorbed or removed.
