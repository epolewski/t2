Cyberdyne

Overview

Cyberdyne Systems Corporation is an organization known for its advanced research and development in AI and robotics technologies.

Dissolution

Cyberdyne's main facility was destroyed during the Terminator 2: Judgement Day events, resulting in the termination of the company.

Legacy

The corporation gained notoriety due to its involvement with Dyson, who created a Prototype AI that eventually led to Skynet's development. The Chip from the first Terminator model was also housed at Cyberdyne, which Terminator used after amputation.
