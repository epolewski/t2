Characterization

SARAH.html">Sarah's father, also known as "the narrator," is an eccentric individual who decorates a metal Pole seasonally and is described as being close to achieving something great in his work on a Neural-net Processor. He appears to be strict and somewhat distant but is supportive of his wife, Tarissa, and their two Children Danny and Blythe.

Family Life

The dad maintains strict rules for his Children, such as only allowing them one crayon at a Time or cupcakes without ice cream for birthdays. This behavior may have contributed to his obsessive nature towards the decorated metal Pole.

Personality Development

Throughout the story, the narrator's father becomes increasingly bizarre, reflecting on his wife who passed away (Mom). The Family dynamics and his obsession with the Pole are explored in detail, shedding light on their lives after her loss.
