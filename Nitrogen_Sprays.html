Nitrogen Sprays

In Terminator 2: Judgment Day, nitrogen sprays were created when a Tanker carrying Liquid Nitrogen exploded in the Steel Mill, causing vapor to spread throughout the facility. The freezing vapors swirled around the area, obscuring visibility and posing a danger to those present.

The nitrogen sprays covered the scene, creating an unusual juxtaposition of Fire and ice as they engulfed the wreckage. The Explosion also left John Connor with a bleeding nose due to the extreme cold, while affecting the T-1000's movements.
