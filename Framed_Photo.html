Framed Photo

In Terminator 2: Judgment Day, a framed photo depicting Dr. Miles Dyson's Family was featured in the Cyberdyne Systems Corporation Building. The photograph displayed his wife and Children, capturing an intimate moment that served as a reminder of the impact of Skynet's actions.

During a chaotic scene involving SWAT teams, explosions, and Gunfire within the Cyberdyne Building, the Glass surrounding the frame shattered as the image was tossed into the central junk pile.
