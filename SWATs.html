SWATs

Depiction in Terminator 2: Judgment Day

In Terminator 2: Judgment Day, SWAT (Special Weapons And Tactics) units are portrayed as a key element in various scenes throughout the film.

Characteristics

During their appearance, SWAT teams can be seen donning Kevlar Vests and carrying M-16 assault rifles, tear gas launchers, and MPK Machine pistols. The teams showcase their expertise in combat situations, with their primary Goal being to locate and neutralize threats posed by Skynet's weapons.

Role in the Film

SWAT units are first introduced when they storm into Cyberdyne Systems Corporation, engaging in a firefight as they advance through the Building and across various locations such as labs and offices. As they attempt to apprehend the main antagonists, they face opposition from T-1000 (the Terminator) and ultimately become trapped themselves due to their aggressive tactics.
