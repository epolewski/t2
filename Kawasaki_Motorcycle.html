Kawasaki Motorcycle

Role in Terminator 2

In Terminator 2: Judgement Day, the Kawasaki motorcycle plays a significant role in high-speed chases and action sequences. Notably, T-1000 uses it to make a dramatic escape from the scene after causing Chaos at Cyberdyne Systems Corporation's headquarters.

Characteristics

The Kawasaki motorcycle is known for its powerful engine and agility, making it an ideal choice for the nimble T-1000 as it weaves through traffic and crashes through Glass windows of office buildings during the pursuit scenes in Terminator 2: Judgement Day
