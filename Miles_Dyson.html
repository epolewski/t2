Miles Dyson

Miles Dyson is an employee of Cyberdyne Systems, working as part of the Special Projects Division.

He is a significant character in the Terminator series due to his involvement in microprocessor designs. His work often causes friction between him and his wife, Tarissa.

Dyson resides in a high-tech luxurious House, where he spends much of his Time working on various computer monitors. He has two Children named Danny and Blythe.
