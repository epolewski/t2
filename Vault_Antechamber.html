Vault Antechamber

In the Terminator series, the Vault Antechamber serves as a secure storage facility containing priceless artifacts related to Skynet's development. It houses relics such as the Artificial Intelligence (A.I.) Lab, Cyberdyne Systems Corporation, and remains of Skynet's construction.

The Vault Antechamber can be found in Pescadero State Hospital for the Criminally Insane and is where John Connor and Miles Dyson stand poised with their keys, signifying their roles in shaping the future of Humanity.

Once the keys are inserted into the Door locks, the Vault Antechamber opens, providing access to the artifacts. This space functions as a silent steel womb that isolates the world outside while safeguarding these critical relics.
