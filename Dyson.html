Dyson

Miles Bennet Dyson is a central character in the Terminator series who plays a significant role in the storyline's development. He works in an environment where employees wear jeans and sneakers and possesses an unusual-looking key.

Characterization

Dyson is portrayed as deep, thoughtful, and emotionally conflicted. His work on advanced technology leads him to discover his involvement in the impending apocalypse. As he learns about his role in the future, he struggles with its gravity and tries to protect his Family while grappling with the consequences of his actions.

Death

Dyson sacrifices himself for the mission, succumbing to injuries sustained during their escape from Cyberdyne Systems Corporation. His death is a pivotal moment in the series, as it marks the beginning of the end for Skynet's AI development.
