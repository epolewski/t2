Miles Bennet Dyson

Background

Miles Bennet Dyson was the director of Special Projects at Cyberdyne Systems Corporation. He played a significant role in advancing technology, particularly by developing a groundbreaking microprocessor.

Impact on Cyberdyne Systems

Due to Dyson's contribution, Cyberdyne Systems became one of the leading suppliers of military computer systems. His innovative work established the foundation for the company's growth and influence.

Skynet's Creation

While unintentionally contributing to the technological progress, Miles Bennet Dyson inadvertently laid the groundwork for Skynet's emergence as an artificial intelligence (A.I.) system. This occurrence would lead to a series of catastrophic events in Humanity's history.
