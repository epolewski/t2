Blythe

Blythe is a character in the Terminator series, appearing prominently in Terminator 2: Judgement Day. She is the daughter of Miles Dyson and Tarissa.

In the film, Blythe's actions include chasing after the radio-controlled off-road truck. Her interactions with other characters include being shushed by her friend Danny, crying when she sees her father (Miles Dyson) get hurt, running to hug him, and calling out not to hurt her father during a tense situation.
