MPK Machine pistol

Description

The MPK Machine pistol is a firearm featured in Terminator 2: Judgment Day. It was used by the Terminator (T-1000) throughout the film, notably during the dramatic Police chase.

Usage

As T-1000 attempts to escape from SWAT officers, he fires his MPK Machine pistol at their Van, causing Bullets to riddle what's left of the Chopper's canopy. This scene highlights the weapon's destructive capabilities and the Terminator's lethal intentions.
