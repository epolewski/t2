Bike Cop

The Bike Cop is a character in the film Terminator 2: Judgement Day. Played by actor David McDaniel, he appears as a Police officer who arrives at the site of an Explosion on his Kawasaki 1100 motorcycle. Throughout the movie, he converses with the T-1000 and engages in multiple encounters with the dangerous cyborg.

Role in the Film

As the situation unfolds amidst the Chaos caused by the T-1000, the Bike Cop navigates through the disorganized crowd and enters the burning Building to search for the enemy. He ultimately plays a crucial role in the pursuit and eventual destruction of the T-1000.
