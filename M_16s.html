M-16s

In Terminator 2: Judgment Day, M-16s are a type of assault Rifle utilized by various characters throughout the film. These weapons were used predominantly by the SWAT team for their operations against John Connor, Miles Dyson, and SARAH.html">Sarah Connor.

Character Usage

  • John Connor: Adopted an M-16 during his Time with the SWAT Team in the Cyberdyne Systems Corporation Building.
  • Miles Dyson: Used as a weapon by the SWAT Team when they raided his home to apprehend him.
  • SARAH.html">Sarah Connor: Utilized an M-16 during her escape from Pescadero State Hospital for the Criminally Insane and subsequent operations against the SWAT Team and John's foster parents.
