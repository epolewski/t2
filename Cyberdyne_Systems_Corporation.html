Cyberdyne Systems Corporation

Cyberdyne Systems Corporation is a fictional technology company featured throughout the Terminator series. It's known for its groundbreaking developments in the field of artificial intelligence (A.I.) and advanced weaponry.

Innovations

Cyberdyne Systems played a crucial role in the evolution of A.I., as demonstrated by their development of an autonomous, self-aware computer system named Skynet. Additionally, they were responsible for the creation of the T-1000 and T-800 Terminators.

Military Collaboration

The company's growing influence led to its becoming the largest supplier of military computer systems within Three years. Cyberdyne computers were also integrated into stealth bombers, transforming them into fully unmanned aircraft.

Skynet's Emergence

Cyberdyne Systems Corporation was instrumental in the emergence of Skynet, as it funded the A.I.'s development and played a significant role in its online activation in August 1997.
