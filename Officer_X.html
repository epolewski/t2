Officer X

In Terminator 2: Judgement Day, Officer X is an investigating officer who visits the Voight residence to inquire about John Connor's whereabouts. He attempts to speak with John but learns he isn't home, and requests a photograph of him.

Additionally, Officer X encounters two young girls near a 7-Eleven store, showing them a picture of John to identify if they have seen him. The first Girl confirms seeing John at the location. Seeking a suspect (John) who was spotted near the Galleria, Officer X approaches Terminator for information on the location of "the what?"

Terminator identifies the place as the Galleria.
