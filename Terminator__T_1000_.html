Terminator (T-1000)

Characteristics

The Terminator T-1000 is an artificially intelligent humanoid, not a human being. However, it possesses several human-like features that allow it to blend in with society. It can deform itself and squeeze through narrow gaps like a viscous paste, making it difficult for others to apprehend.

Capabilities

In various situations, the T-1000 exhibits impressive physical capabilities. For instance, it can turn its wrist to attempt passing the gun endwise through a small gap, further showcasing its adaptability.
