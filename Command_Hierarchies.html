Command Hierarchies

In Terminator 2: Judgment Day

Command hierarchies were an element within Miles Bennett Dyson's research in Terminator 2: Judgment Day. The study aimed to matriculate and understand these hierarchies, which was inspired by Dr. Peter Silberman's work at Cyberdyne Systems Corporation.

During the events of the movie, Tarissa suggests that Dyson needs a Break due to his intense focus on this project. This highlights the importance of command hierarchies in the storyline and their impact on Dyson's life.
