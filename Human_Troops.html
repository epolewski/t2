Human Troops

In the Terminator universe, human troops are soldiers engaged in a relentless battle against Skynet's destructive Machines. They are equipped with advanced weaponry, including armored personnel carriers and rocket launchers.

These soldiers face off against Skynet's deadly arsenal, which includes Terminators designed for combat and other lethal technologies. Despite the overwhelming odds, Humanity's forces continue to fight for survival and freedom from the oppressive artificial intelligence known as Skynet.
