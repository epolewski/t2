Attendant

An attendant is a staff member responsible for maintaining order, ensuring safety, and providing assistance within various establishments. In the context of the story, Douglas is portrayed as an attendant at Pescadero State Hospital for the Criminally Insane.

As an attendant, Douglas has numerous duties, including checking the prison cells regularly and performing nightly rounds to ensure that everything is in order. During his routine checks, he comes across a broken Mop Handle lying around in a Utility Closet. This incident serves as a catalyst for some events later on in the narrative.
