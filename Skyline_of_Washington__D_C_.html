Skyline of Washington, D.C.

The Skyline of Washington, D.C. is characterized by iconic structures such as the Capitol Building, the Washington Monument, and a variety of advanced high-rise buildings that have contributed to its evolving landscape over Time.

  • Washington Monument

Architectural Development

Over the years, new constructions and architectural innovations have added to the skyline of Washington, D.C., shaping its present appearance.
