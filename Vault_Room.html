Vault Room

In the context of most organizations, a vault room is a highly secure area where valuable items or Documents are stored. Access to this location typically requires specific authorization and often involves the use of two unique keys.

In the realm of cybernetics and artificial intelligence, the Vault Room in the Cyberdyne Systems Corporation was responsible for housing artifacts that could potentially be used as precursors to advanced technology.

The contents of the Cyberdyne Systems Vault Room included stainless steel cabinets holding various artifacts, such as a ceramic rectangle and a damaged Metal Hand and forearm. These items were crucial in understanding the capabilities of Skynet's AI.
