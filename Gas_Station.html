Gas Station

A gas station is a place where vehicles can refuel and purchase automotive supplies. These stations typically feature a buzzing neon sign, a pump for fueling cars, and a small food stand offering quick bites.

In this specific case, the gas station is rundown with a buzzing neon sign that operates daily except on Sundays. The fast-food stand offers sleazy meals enjoyed by families seated at picnic tables set up alongside the food stand.
