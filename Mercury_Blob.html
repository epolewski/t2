Mercury Blob

Characteristics

A Mercury Blob is a key feature of Skynet's advanced Terminator models, particularly the T-1000. This shape-shifting substance allows the Machine to travel through Time and adapt to various environments. It has the ability to separate into smaller droplets and reintegrate with the main mass when needed.

Functionality

The primary function of the Mercury Blob is to enable the T-1000 to blend in with its surroundings. When dispersed, it can mimic surrounding objects or even merge with other materials, making it difficult for humans to identify the Terminator as an artificial entity.
