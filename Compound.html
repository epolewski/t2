Compound

In the Terminator series, a compound is a location where characters gather and strategize. It typically serves as a base of operations for resistance members against Skynet's forces.

  • Pescadero State Hospital for the Criminally Insane - A maximum-security wing in Terminator 2: Judgement Day, where SARAH.html">Sarah Connor is held captive.
  • Salceda's Compound - In Terminator 3: Rise of the Machines, a group led by Enrique Salceda operates from this compound to fight Skynet's forces.
  • Cyberdyne Systems Corporation - The main setting in Terminator Salvation, where the resistance fights against Skynet and its human collaborators.
