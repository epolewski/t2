Ready-Teller Machine

Function

A Ready-Teller Machine is an automated teller Machine used in banks to dispense cash upon inputting the correct personal identification number (PIN). These Machines are designed to provide customers with quick access to their funds without needing direct interaction with a bank employee.

Usage in the Story

In the narrative, John Connor uses a stolen ATM Card to insert it into the Ready-Teller Machine at a Local Bank. This action plays a significant role in his efforts to gather resources and evade capture from the authorities.
