Voight House

In Terminator 2: Judgment Day, the Voight House serves as a location of significant importance for protagonist John Connor's mission. It is here that he seeks refuge, but is urged by his Terminator ally not to do so, citing potential dangers from the T-1000 threat.

The events at the Voight House are intense and include Janelle impaling Todd Voight with a Knife in the Kitchen.

The Backyard also plays a role in the unfolding story, adding to the tension as the characters navigate their way through this critical setting.
