Window Woman

The Window Woman is a character who works at the ordering window of a Gas Station in the movie Terminator 2: Judgment Day. She plays a minor role, primarily interacting with protagonist John Connor while he attempts to purchase food from her.

During their interaction, John tries to smile and lighten up the mood, which garners a critical response from the Window Woman. This exchange showcases his struggle to maintain a sense of normalcy amidst the Chaos surrounding him.
