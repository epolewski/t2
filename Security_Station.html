Security Station

In the Terminator universe, a security station is a location where security personnel are tasked with monitoring and controlling the various systems within the Building they oversee. The term can refer to a specific area or room equipped with multiple monitors, allowing them to observe and manage different aspects of the premises.

In Terminator 2: Judgement Day, the security station serves as a crucial setting for Miles Dyson's mission to access and manipulate Cyberdyne Systems' artificial intelligence lab. It features a locker that Dyson attempts to open in his quest to prevent Skynet from becoming self-aware.
