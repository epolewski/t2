Battlefield

In the Terminator series, the term "battlefield" usually refers to a nighttime setting where the human resistance fights against Skynet's robotic forces. The battlefields typically feature ruined urban environments, with humans utilizing armored personnel carriers as their primary mode of transportation.

These settings showcase the desperate struggle between humans and Machines in a post-apocalyptic world. The backdrop is characterized by exploding buildings, fires, and intense combat scenarios. Ruins of structures offer cover for the guerilla army while they engage in life-or-death confrontations with the enemy Machines.
