Tanker

Overview

A tanker is a large vehicle designed for transportation of liquids or other materials. In this scene, it plays a crucial role in chasing down the Pickup truck at high speed.

Role in the Scene

The tanker aggressively pursues the Pickup truck, attempting to slam into and damage the smaller vehicle. Ultimately, it succeeds in sandwiching the Pickup between itself and the center divider, causing significant damage to the Pickup truck.
