Smelter Crucible

A smelter crucible is an area within a smelting facility where molten steel is processed and stored. In the context of the Terminator 2: Judgement Day film, John Connor finds his sister, SARAH.html">Sarah, near one of these crucibles as she limps towards him.

John Connor emerges from the shadows onto a landing adjacent to the smelter crucible, marking an important moment in their journey together.
