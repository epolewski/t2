Tanker Truck

The Tanker truck is an essential vehicle in the movie Terminator 2: Judgement Day. It has brakes that harden, groaning and shuddering when applied. It stops just short of a wrecked Helicopter, with large blue letters on its side reading "CRYOCO INC. Liquid Nitrogen SUPPLY." This Tanker truck later accelerates and pushes the Helicopter wreckage aside.

In the film's narrative, the Tanker Truck plays a vital role in the action sequences involving Liquid Nitrogen production at the Cyberdyne Systems Corporation plant in Washington D.C.
