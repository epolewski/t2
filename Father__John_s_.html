Father (John's)

Characterization

The father of John Connor in the Terminator series imparts valuable wisdom upon his son.

Message

In the first film, Terminator 1, the father teaches John the message "The future is not set. There is no fate but what we make for ourselves." This philosophy becomes a guiding principle for John throughout the franchise.
