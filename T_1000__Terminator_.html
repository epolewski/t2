T-1000 (Terminator)

The T-1000 (also known as "Terminator") is an unstoppable cyborg assassin featured in Terminator 2: Judgement Day. In one scene, the T-1000 drives a Pickup truck with John Connor as his passenger while attempting to evade a pursuing Tanker. During the chase, he manages to push the Pickup between the Tanker and the center divider, ultimately transferring control of the vehicle to John.
