Pickup Truck

The Pickup truck is an essential vehicle featured throughout the Terminator series. In Terminator 2: Judgement Day, it is an old Chevy Pickup with a heavy home-made wooden camper shell that John and Terminator use for their escape from the City.

When they start the truck, Terminator breaks into the car by breaking the steering column. During their escape attempt, the truck faces significant damage from the pursuing Tanker, resulting in the disintegration of its wooden camper.
