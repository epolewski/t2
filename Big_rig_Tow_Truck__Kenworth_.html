Big-rig Tow Truck (Kenworth)

In Terminator 2: Judgement Day, the Big-rig Tow Truck (a Kenworth model) is a significant vehicle used by the antagonist, the Terminator T-1000. As a large and powerful tow truck, this vehicle possesses considerable force and agility.

During the climactic chase scene in the film, the Big-rig Tow Truck can be seen crashing through a fence and launching into the center of a Canal. The truck's intimidating appearance, combined with its speed and power, adds an element of dread to the cinematic experience.
