HE Grenades

Introduction

HE grenades are a type of explosive ordnance used in the Terminator franchise. They are characterized by their ability to cause significant damage upon detonation.

Usage

In the films, HE grenades are primarily utilized by the protagonist, John Connor. To activate an HE Grenade, the user must snap it closed with a wrist flip before throwing it at the intended target.

Effects

When detonated, HE grenades release an Explosion that can inflict substantial destruction on its surroundings. They have been featured in several key moments throughout the Terminator series, showcasing their potency as a weapon of choice for characters in this universe.
