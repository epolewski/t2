Card

The card is an essential component in the lives of two young boys named John and Tim. It serves as a key tool for their illegal activities involving theft from Ready-Teller Machines at local banks.

In this story, John Connor, a ten-year-old prodigy with advanced technological knowledge, uses the card to access the Black-Box Electronics Unit. The Plasma Screen on the card displays the PIN number required for successful transactions.

John and Tim, friends who are engaged in electronic age thievery, make use of technology like the card, Black-Box Electronics Unit, and laptop keyboard to commit these bank robberies. While Tim is amazed by John's skills, he plays an important role as well.
