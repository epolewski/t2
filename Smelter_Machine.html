Smelter Machine

A smelter Machine is an industrial apparatus used for grinding and processing various materials, typically found in large-scale operations like steel mills. In Terminator 2: Judgement Day, it plays a significant role in the storyline as John Connor and the Terminator navigate through its dangerous environment.

Due to the extreme heat generated by these Machines, they create a hallucinatory effect on the surroundings, making navigation even more challenging for the characters.
