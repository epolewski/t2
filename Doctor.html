Doctor

The doctor is a character who played a role in observing the Terminator. Throughout his interactions with the Terminator, he noticed several distinct differences between it and a typical human being. These observations led him to conclude that the Terminator was not a living creature.

During an encounter with the Terminator, the doctor experienced feelings of being lifted off the ground, despite remaining stationary. This sensation occurred when the Terminator was hit by a wall, causing him to understand that the Terminator's actions were not influenced by gravity or physical limitations.
