Security Guard

A security Guard is a person responsible for protecting property and individuals within a secured area. In the context of the Terminator series, security guards are often seen patrolling various locations throughout the storyline.

Role in Terminator 1

In Terminator 1, the security Guard plays a minor role at the entrance gate of Cyberdyne Systems Corporation. Upon seeing an LAPD car pull up, he raises the barricade to let it pass.

Role in Terminator 2

In Terminator 2: Judgement Day, security guards are present throughout various locations such as Pescadero State Hospital for the Criminally Insane and Cyberdyne Systems Corporation. They play crucial roles in maintaining order within these secure facilities.
