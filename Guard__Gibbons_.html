Guard (Gibbons)

In the Terminator universe, the character Guard (Gibbons) is an employee who works at the front desk of Cyberdyne Systems Laboratory.

During his tenure at the laboratory, Gibbons refers to Dr. Miles Bennet Dyson as "Mr. Dyson" and adheres strictly to the visitor rules enforced in the facility.
