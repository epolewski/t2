SWAT Troopers

SWAT troopers are highly trained and skillful law enforcement agents who are equipped with advanced weaponry and protective gear. They typically wear body armor and gas masks while carrying M-16 assault rifles and tear-gas launchers on their missions.

During their operations, SWAT troopers often enter buildings using ropes to scale walls or windows, breaking in through any means necessary.

In a typical mission, SWAT troopers will Fire tear-gas grenades into second-floor offices through broken windows to gain control of the situation.
