Beretta Pistol

In Terminator 2: Judgment Day, a Beretta Pistol was used as a weapon by the Police officer attempting to apprehend John Connor and his foster parents. The Cop fired the gun at the advancing Terminator (T-1000) but failed to harm it due to its superior resilience.

During the confrontation, the Cop emptied the pistol twice in an attempt to fend off the relentless Terminator. However, the ammunition proved futile against the indestructible Machine.
