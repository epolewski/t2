Police

In Terminator 2: Judgment Day, police officers arrive at the Cyberdyne Systems Corporation Building following an emergency call.

Upon arrival, they quickly disembark from their vehicles and take cover behind them. As they approach the Building, a SWAT Team is also dispatched to assist in handling the situation.

Meanwhile, a Helicopter arrives with a searchlight equipped with Xenon technology, which is used to illuminate the area around the Building.
