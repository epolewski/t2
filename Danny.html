Danny

Danny is a character in the movie Terminator 2: Judgement Day. He is the son of Miles Dyson and Tarissa">Tarissa. In the film, he is portrayed as a young child who enjoys playing with radio-controlled off-road trucks.

Characteristics

In his interactions with the protagonists, Danny demonstrates a mature demeanor and communicates using finger gestures to signal for silence. At one point in the story, Terminator asks him to retrieve bandages from the Bathroom.
