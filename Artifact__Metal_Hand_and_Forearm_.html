Artifact (Metal Hand and Forearm)

The Artifact (Metal Hand and Forearm) is a piece of machinery with an intricately designed Metal Hand and forearm. The object features a metallic surface that appears scorched and discolored, showcasing the damages it has sustained over Time.

In its design, the elbow area exhibits twisted and crushed metal, giving it a unique appearance. This artifact was discovered in the Vault Room alongside other objects related to the history of Skynet's AI development at Cyberdyne Systems Corporation.
