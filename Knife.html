Knife

In Terminator 2: Judgment Day, a knife with an eight-inch blade was used by the third Biker during his attack on the T-1000 (also known as Terminator). The scene occurs at the Biker's hangout, where the Biker attempts to stab Terminator. However, Terminator quickly grabs the handle of the knife and pins the Biker to the bar top.

Usage

The knife played a significant role in the encounter between the Biker and Terminator, showcasing the quick reflexes and strength of the powerful cyborg.
