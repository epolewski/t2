Todd Voight

Todd Voight is a middle-aged working man and the husband of Janella. In the narrative, he doesn't seem to be deeply involved in John Connor's life. He is the legal guardian of John, following his parents' death.

In Terminator 2: Judgement Day, Todd tries to get John to comply with Janella's request regarding some unspecified matter. As part of the storyline, he is concerned when Janelle mentions another visitor asking about John. His primary role is to provide support and guidance as John's guardian.

Todd Voight is not a prominent character within the narrative, often seen coming home from work, ignoring Janelle, and opening the fridge or growling at the dog barking. He is pinned to a Kitchen cabinet by Janelle/T-1000's spiked arm and later killed by her.
