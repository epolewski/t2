I-Beam

An I-beam is a critical component in manufacturing industries and construction projects. It serves as a structural support for buildings, bridges, and other heavy structures.

In the context of the Terminator franchise, an I-beam plays a significant role when the T-1000 uses it as a weapon against the original Terminator. This showcases the versatility and durability of the metal alloy employed in creating these beams.

Function

I-beams are utilized to lift ingots into smelters and run on linear tracks. They provide structural support for various industrial and construction applications.
