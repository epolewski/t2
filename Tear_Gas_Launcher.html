Tear-Gas Launcher

Characteristics

The tear-gas launcher is a rotary weapon capable of holding 12 rounds in a large drum. It is most notably used by the Terminator character in the storyline.

Application

In the narrative, the tear-gas launcher is utilized by the Terminator against SWAT members during an assault on Cyberdyne Systems' headquarters. The device fires gas canisters into the Lobby, causing pandemonium and temporarily disabling several SWAT Team members.
