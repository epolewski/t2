Sally-Port Corridor

Definition

A sally-port Corridor is a small passageway specifically designed to control and restrict movement of potentially violent individuals within an institution. It serves as a security measure to prevent escape attempts by inmates.

Usage

In Terminator 2: Judgement Day, the sally-port Corridor appears as part of the Pescadero State Hospital for the Criminally Insane, where it was utilized to ensure the safety of patients and staff. The T-1000/Guard passes through this area undetected, allowing him to continue his mission without drawing significant attention from the Hospital Attendants.
