Blooper

In the Terminator series, a blooper is a versatile weapon utilized by the Terminators. It can function both as a whip and a lasso, allowing for efficient maneuverability in combat situations.

During the chase scene in Terminator 2: Judgement Day, the T-1000 Terminator (played by Robert Patrick) demonstrates the use of his blooper. He unslings it from his neck and secures his target with it, ultimately replacing the Grenade he had previously picked up.
