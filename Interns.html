Interns

In the narrative of Terminator 2: Judgment Day, Dr. Peter Silberman's interns are a group of young individuals who participate in his studies on mental health issues. They frequently accompany him during discussions, showing their agreement through nonverbal cues such as nodding.

Role in the Story

The interns serve to establish the context and atmosphere in which Dr. Silberman operates, providing insight into his professional environment at Pescadero State Hospital for the Criminally Insane.
