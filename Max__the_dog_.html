Max (the dog)

Max is a character in the Terminator series who appears as John Connor's German Shepherd dog. He is portrayed by an actual canine actor, Wolfy.

Max plays a significant role in the storyline, primarily serving as a source of comfort and companionship for John Connor during his encounters with various Terminators and battles against Skynet's weapons. Max is also known to bark at potential threats, such as Janelle Voight when she approaches him, highlighting his protective nature towards John.

Appearance

In Terminator 2: Judgement Day, Max appears as a German Shepherd dog owned by John Connor (played by Edward Furlong). He shares the screen with the other characters throughout the film.
