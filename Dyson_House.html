Dyson House

Dyson House is a location in the Terminator series, serving as the workplace and residence of Dr. Miles Dyson.

In the film Terminator 2: Judgement Day, the T-1000, a Terminator model with advanced shape-shifting abilities, searches through its office here. After the Explosion caused by the T-1000's destruction, the House becomes deserted.

The Police walkie-talkie used by the T-1000 blares a message about an ongoing situation at Cyberdyne Systems Corporation, another significant location in the series. This connection underscores the central role of Dyson House within the narrative.
