Biker

Characteristics

The biker is a character in the Terminator series, often associated with his iconic Harley Davidson motorcycle and cigar. He is known for carrying a .45 auto tucked under his leather Jacket.

During an encounter with the Terminator, he struggles to hold onto his weapon due to pain from previous injuries, eventually getting disarmed by the relentless cyborg. The biker moans when the Terminator jerks out the Knife from his back.

Episodes

The biker can be seen in various episodes of the Terminator series, such as in a truck stop scene where he is pinned to a pool table.
