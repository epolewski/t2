Truckers

In the Terminator universe, truckers are a subculture found within the world of long-haul truck drivers. These individuals often gather at truck stops to socialize, eat, and engage in various activities.

Description

At a truck stop diner, truckers can be seen sitting around eating chili-sizes and wearing CAT hats pushed back on their heads. They are known for staring at any unusual events unfolding around them, such as when a naked man enters the establishment.
