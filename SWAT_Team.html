SWAT Team

In Terminator 2: Judgement Day, a SWAT team is depicted as dealing with their weapons and observing the Terminator emerge from the smoke. They appear tense due to seeing something they've never encountered before.

During the encounter, the SWAT team fires at the Terminator. As a result, several members get shot or injured; one member is hit in the chest, another in the back, one in the leg, and two more in their legs. The team's gas masks are removed by the Terminator, causing them to retreat after being affected by the tear gas.
