Steel Mill

In Terminator 2: Judgement Day, the Steel Mill is a setting that features prominently in the climactic chase sequence between Terminator (T-1000) and John Connor. The steel mill's gates are destroyed during this high-speed pursuit, adding to the chaotic atmosphere.

The Steel Mill forms part of an area of heavy industry. It serves as a crucial location where the characters find themselves after the crash into the Building, leading to a dramatic finale involving Fire, explosions, and nitrogen vapor. This industrial backdrop adds a distinctive element to the final scene of the film.
