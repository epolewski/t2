Honda

In Terminator Franchise

In the Terminator franchise, Honda appears as John Connor's motorcycle. The Bike plays a significant role in the film series, showcasing John's rebellious nature and his connection to the modern world.

In "Missile Command"

Honda is also part of the classic arcade game Missile Command, where it represents one of the ICBMs (Intercontinental Ballistic Missiles), MIRVs (Multiple Independently Targetable Reentry Vehicles), or warheads that the player must defend against incoming missile attacks.
