Polaroid

Significance

In the Terminator universe, a Polaroid is an important keepsake for characters John Connor and Kyle Reese. The photograph features SARAH.html">Sarah Connor, pregnant, in a jeep near the Mexican border.

The Polaroid photograph was initially carried by John Connor throughout his journey, providing him with a connection to his mother's past and the struggle against Skynet. After John's death, he passed it on to Kyle Reese, who continued to protect their shared legacy.
