The Wall of Nitrogen Vapor

In Terminator 2: Judgment Day, the "Wall of Nitrogen Vapor" is an important location within the film. It showcases a unique blend of Fire and ice, as the flames from the Steel Mill intermingle with the nitrogen vapor.

This phenomenon occurs when the T-1000 (played by Robert Patrick) encounters the wall, causing it to freeze him solid into a statue. Once the threat has been neutralized, the vapor evaporates, allowing the frozen Terminator to resume normal functioning.
