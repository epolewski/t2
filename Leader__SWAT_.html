Leader (SWAT)

Role in Terminator 2: Judgment Day

In the film Terminator 2: Judgment Day, the Leader of the SWAT Team is responsible for directing his unit during their mission to apprehend the T-1000, a shape-shifting cyborg assassin. He communicates orders through a megaphone and coordinates the team's efforts to stop the Terminator.

Reaction to the Terminator

The Leader of the SWAT Team is astonished when he realizes that Bullets have no effect on the seemingly indestructible T-1000, highlighting the uniqueness of this advanced killing Machine in contrast to traditional law enforcement tools and tactics.
