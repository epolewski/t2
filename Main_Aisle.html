Main Aisle

In Terminator 2: Judgment Day, the Main Aisle serves as a key location. It is the primary space or area within the parking Garage where John Connor (played by Edward Furlong) rides out on his Bike, looking back to see the T-1000 (portrayed by Robert Patrick) running towards him.

Additionally, the Main Aisle is present in the Steel Mill scene, where the Terminator (T-1000), played by Robert Patrick, slams onto the floor after leaping from the cab during the intense chase sequence involving the truck and the Helicopter.
