Canal

In the Terminator film series, the canal is an integral part of the high-speed chase route taken by various characters. It features prominently during the pursuit sequences and helps to define the dynamic nature of the action-packed scenes.

The canal borders a dirt path, providing ample space for vehicles to navigate through the area. Its most notable feature is the tunnel that the truck being chased cannot fit or stop in, leading to a suspenseful scene as the heroes attempt to outrun their relentless pursuer.
