Bryant

Character Overview

Bryant is a young laboratory assistant who works alongside Miles Dyson at Cyberdyne Systems in Terminator 2: Judgement Day. He demonstrates respect for his superior, referring to him as "Mr. Dyson." Curious about the mysterious origin of an artifact found within the company's possession, Bryant accompanies Dyson into the Vault Room when he investigates it.

During the visit to the vault, Bryant stays outside with a Guard while Dyson enters. His role primarily revolves around assisting in research and observing the discoveries made by his colleagues in The Lab.
