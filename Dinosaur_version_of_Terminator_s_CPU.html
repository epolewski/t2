Dinosaur version of Terminator's CPU

In the Terminator universe, a dinosaur version of the Terminator's central processing unit (CPU) was discovered by Dr. Miles Bennet Dyson in his research at Cyberdyne Systems Corporation. This artifact, which resembles a metal box assembly with small cubes, was found as part of an experiment dating back to the Cretaceous period.

Dr. Dyson aimed to harness the power of this primitive artificial intelligence, intending to use it for military applications, but realized that its output rapidly degraded after just Three seconds of operation. This discovery led him to question the feasibility and morality of such advanced AI technology.
