Orderly

An orderly is a person working within a healthcare institution, usually responsible for maintaining order and assisting with patient care.

Role in the Story

In the story of Terminator 2: Judgement Day, the Orderlies play a minor role as they work at the Nurse's station and attempt to use a stunner on SARAH.html">Sarah. However, she overpowers them using a baton.

Later, a group of Orderlies are seen chasing SARAH.html">Sarah in an effort to catch her. They try to unlock a Door using their keys but fail due to SARAH.html">Sarah breaking off her key in the lock. Silberman shouts at them to go around the obstacle.

Additional Roles

In addition to their primary duties, Orderlies also assist in subduing Doctor Silberman during the chaotic events that unfold within the mental health facility.
