Janelle

Janelle is a character in the Terminator series, portrayed by actress S. Epatha Merkerson.

Character Traits

Janelle is depicted as an individual who cares about John Connor's well-being and often receives calls from his "Terminator" (T-800). She expresses concern for her brother Todd Voight and inquires about John's whereabouts.

Powers and Abilities

In one scene, Janelle demonstrates her unique ability to transform her arm into a metal cylinder that can turn into a spike or revert back into a hand. This capability indicates that she is also a T-1000 model, like the character T-1000 in Terminator 2: Judgement Day.

Janelle kills Todd Voight and assumes his identity as a Police officer (T-1000), further showcasing her adaptability and versatility as a Terminator.
