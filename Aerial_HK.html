Aerial HK

Aerial HK refers to a series of flying HK combat drones, which are part of Skynet's vast weaponry arsenal. These autonomous aerial vehicles are deployed during battles against human forces.

The Aerial HKs play an essential role in Skynet's strategy, often targeting and taking down enemy aircraft or engaging in direct assaults on ground troops. However, they can be neutralized by well-armed human resistance fighters equipped with Anti-Tank Guided Missiles (LAW rockets) mounted on armored personnel carriers (APC).
