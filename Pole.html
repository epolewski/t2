Pole

Characteristics

The pole is made of metal and serves as a decorative element in the yard. It can be shaped like a crucifix or other symbolic figures depending on the occasion.

Usage

The pole is used during various holidays to represent different characters such as Santa Claus, a soldier, Uncle Sam, and a ghost. During Thanksgiving night, it wears a Santa suit; for Veteran's Day, it dresses up as a soldier; on the Fourth of July, it transforms into Uncle Sam; and during Halloween, it becomes a ghost.

Significance

The pole holds sentimental value for the Family members, serving as a symbol of remembrance for their late mother after her passing. It is also used by the father to express his feelings through strings attached to it with index cards containing messages.

Disposal

When the House is sold to a young couple, they remove the pole and sticks, leaving them by the road on garbage day.
