Chopper

The Helicopter, also known as a "chopper," plays a significant role in Terminator 2: Judgement Day. It arrives at Cyberdyne Systems Corporation's headquarters and swings into the lot. The chopper then rakes its XENON SPOTLIGHT through the second floor offices.

The T-1000 (Terminator) takes control of the Helicopter, forcing the pilot out of the cockpit. As it loses control due to damage, the chopper spins out of control before auto-rotating to land. Once near the ground, the T-1000 regains control, hovering ten feet above the parking lot.
