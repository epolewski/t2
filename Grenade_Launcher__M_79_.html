Grenade Launcher (M-79)

A Grenade Launcher, also known as an M-79, is a one-handed weapon designed for firing grenades. In Terminator 2: Judgement Day, this particular weapon was utilized by the Terminator (T-800 Model 101) during his high-speed chase with the T-1000.

The M-79 played a crucial role in the conflict between the two Terminators, as it enabled the T-800 to reposition and defend himself against the relentless pursuit of the T-1000. Throughout the chase, the T-800 continuously reloaded and fired multiple grenades at the T-1000, showcasing its effectiveness in combat situations.
