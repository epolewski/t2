Tourist

In Terminator 2: Judgment Day, a Japanese Tourist is present during the climactic events at Cyberdyne Systems Corporation. The character is portrayed as an unsuspecting individual who happens to be in the wrong place at the right Time.

The tourist encounters a shocking scene when he steps forward to capture a photograph of John Connor's dead body, which lies atop T-1000 (the Terminator). He captures this image while the other characters remain oblivious to his presence.
