CPU Chip

A CPU Chip is a reddish-brown ceramic rectangle with a connector on one end. This artifact plays a significant role in understanding the nature and origin of Skynet's Terminators.

The Chip was discovered by John Connor when he broke into Cyberdyne Systems to retrieve critical evidence. The Chip, once removed from a Terminator, offered crucial insights about the technology behind these artificially intelligent beings. It revealed that they were cyborgs made up of living tissue over a metal endoskeleton, controlled by advanced artificial intelligence.
