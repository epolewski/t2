Side Street

The Side Street is a significant location in the movie Terminator 2: Judgment Day. It played a crucial role as the path taken by the main characters, Terminator (T-800) and John Connor, while escaping from the disastrous scene of a nuclear Explosion.

In the movie, the Side Street was depicted as an empty street at night. As the protagonists fled through it, they managed to evade the hazardous aftermath of the catastrophic event, which involved a massive nuclear Explosion in Los Angeles, California.
