Lloyd

Characteristics

Lloyd is the owner of the truck stop diner, where various characters like Truckers, Pool Players, and bikers gather for food and relaxation.

Involvement in the Story

Throughout the story, Lloyd faces several challenging situations, including a naked Terminator calmly walking through his diner. He also tries to prevent the Terminator from taking away another person's Bike, but eventually succumbs to fear and hesitation when deciding whether or not to kill the Terminator.

Conclusion

As the events unfold, Lloyd grows increasingly anxious and sweats profusely. Eventually, he realizes that he is outmatched by the unstoppable nature of the Terminator and gives him his sunglasses before accepting defeat.
