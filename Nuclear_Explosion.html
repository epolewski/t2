Nuclear Explosion

In the Terminator universe, a nuclear Explosion is a recurring event that has substantial impact on both characters and environments. It can be seen in various forms throughout different films in the series.

Legacy

The impact of nuclear explosions on characters serves as a reminder of the dangers posed by Skynet's weapons and the potential consequences faced by Humanity in its fight against the artificial intelligence system.
