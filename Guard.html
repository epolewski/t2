Guard

Characterization

In both Terminator 1 and Terminator 2, the term "guard" is used to refer to individuals who perform security duties, such as maintaining order in high-security facilities or protecting valuable items. They play crucial roles in the storylines of these films.

Functions

Security guards monitor restricted areas, ensure compliance with rules and regulations, and safeguard People and assets within their jurisdiction. Their primary responsibilities include patrolling assigned areas, monitoring surveillance systems, and responding to alarms or emergencies.

Appearances

In Terminator 1, guards are present at Pescadero State Hospital for the Criminally Insane, where they accompany Miles Dyson during his visit to the Artificial Intelligence (A.I.) Lab and oversee Bryant in the Vault Room. In Terminator 2, a guard is seen locking the Door behind other Cops when they leave the Hospital. Later, this same guard discovers T-1000 but is unable to move due to its solid metal object.
