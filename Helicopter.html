Helicopter

In Terminator 2: Judgement Day, a helicopter serves as a mode of transportation for the antagonist, the T-1000. It is utilized by the cyborg during its pursuit of the protagonists, SARAH.html">Sarah and John Connor.

During one scene, the T-1000 manages to crash into the canopy of a Building after causing damage to a SWAT Van. This collision disintegrates the helicopter on impact, leaving behind wreckage and significant damage to the Van's right front fender.
