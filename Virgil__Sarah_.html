Virgil (SARAH.html">Sarah)

In the Terminator 2: Judgment Day, Virgil is an alternate personification of SARAH.html">Sarah Connor. This character is brought to life by the cybernetic organism known as T-1000, who takes on the form of SARAH.html">Sarah's close friend, Virgil. Throughout the story, Terminator and Virgil encounter various challenges in their mission to stop Skynet's development.

Nuclear Explosion

During a tour of nuclear-age Hell (Inferno), Virgil experiences a nuclear Explosion alongside Terminator. In this scene, they both witness the devastating impact of the detonation firsthand.

Charcoal Statue

Upon witnessing the destructive force of the Explosion, Virgil transforms into a charcoal statue and disintegrates into black leaves of ash, symbolizing the destruction wrought by the nuclear blast.
