Attendants

In the Terminator series, attendants are individuals who serve various functions within institutional settings such as hospitals and psychiatric facilities.

Pescadero State Hospital

At Pescadero State Hospital for the Criminally Insane, attendants can be seen standing nearby in the Interview Room, observing and attending to Video SARAH.html">Sarah's wellbeing. They help restrain her when she becomes uncontrollable during interviews.

Security Checkpoint

Additionally, Security Checkpoint attendants work at the facility entrance, monitoring the Corridor for unauthorized entry. Their role includes unlocking Doors and ensuring safety within the premises.

In Terminator 2: Judgement Day, Three Orderly attendants are involved in the situation at Pescadero State Hospital. One of them hits the panic button to alert other staff members.
