Fire

In the Terminator universe, fire is an omnipresent element that symbolizes destruction, Chaos, and the relentless pursuit of Skynet. It serves as a destructive force capable of causing significant damage to environments and People.

Representation

Throughout the series, fire plays a role in various scenes, including Terminator 1 and Terminator 2: Judgement Day. In these instances, it contributes to the destruction caused by Skynet's weapons and the Terminators themselves.

Effects on Characters
