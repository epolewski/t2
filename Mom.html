Mom

Characterization

Mom, or SARAH.html">Sarah Connor, is the mother of John Connor and a crucial figure in the Terminator series. She has passed away, but remains influential in the lives of her Family members. The characters remember her through their experiences with her and various photographs found by the Terminator.

Parental Role

Mom's presence is felt throughout the story as she helps guide her Children in their struggle against Skynet and its relentless pursuit to eliminate them. Her death affects SARAH.html">Sarah and Reese's relationship and leaves an emotional impact on the storyline.

Legacy

Mom's influence can be seen not only through the actions of her Family members but also through her letters, photos, and messages that they have received. These artifacts serve as a connection to her memory and life experiences. The Terminator, as well as John Connor, carry these tokens with them throughout their journey.
