Harley

The Harley is a custom Electro-Glide motorcycle, which serves as a mode of transportation in the film Terminator 2: Judgement Day. It was initially stolen from a character named Lloyd.

In the film, this Harley is a key asset to John and Terminator's escape from pursuers. The Bike features a 10-gauge Shotgun mounted on it for added firepower. Riding the Harley, Terminator weaves through traffic and races down service ramps with great speed and agility, making it advantageous over the big-rig tow truck that the T-1000 uses.

The Harley plays a crucial role in their escape as it gets smashed against a wall while Terminator throws it at the T-1000, causing significant damage to the enemy Machine.
